Fixed Wing 2018

As Project Lead I'm in charge of overall system architecture, power plant design, controls, and aerodynamics for the OS-3 Raven. With a team of about 10 students, we designed this plane for the SAE Aero Micro class in 2018, but weren't able to compete. We have continued to develop the plane and refine the design in the hope of using the lessons we've learned in next year's competition.

My Contributions

  • Chose airfoils for the highest Lift/Drag ratio at low Reynolds Number (1000-5000) due to small scale wings.
  • Designed wings and wing mounts for 3D print.
  • Removed material and mass from 3D printed shapes in order to keep plane lightweight.
  • Designed parts to be quickly and easily removed from chassis and to change position along length of plane for CG adjustment.
