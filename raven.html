Fixed Wing 2019

As Project Lead I'm in charge of overall system architecture, power plant design, controls, and aerodynamics for the OS-3 Raven. With a team of about 10 students, we designed this plane for the SAE Aero Micro class in 2018, but weren't able to compete. The challenge for this competition is to carry as much PVC payload as possible while remaining lightweight and easily assembled. We have continued to develop the plane and refine the design for the 2019 Competition. We attended the 2019 SAE Aero Design East competition in May 2019.

Design

  • Chose airfoils for the highest Lift/Drag ratio at low Reynolds Number (1000-5000) due to small scale wings.
  • Designed wings and wing mounts in SOLIDWORKS for 3D print.
  • Removed material and mass from 3D printed shapes in order to keep plane lightweight.
  • Designed parts to be quickly and easily removed from chassis and to change position along length of plane for CG adjustment.
